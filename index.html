Soldering Sculpture

Task: Fabricate a small sculpture by soldering 12 pins and a bent brass rod on a protoboard
Materials: protoboard, 12 pin header, brass rod, soldering iron

The sculpture I created by bending the brass rod with a tweezer is a tree-like shape. For every assignment and skill I learn in this course, I want to think of it as planting a tree and creating a forest of knowledege. I visited the lab during office hours to learn how to solder with the help of TAs. I became familiar with the tools and practiced soldering with the 10 pin heads and 3 points on the sculpture.
